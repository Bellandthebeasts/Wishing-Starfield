Quick TOS

  • Artwork commissioned from me may not be used commercially, for AI, or NFTs.
  • I use Discord to communicate; my username is BeastBell
  • I will send you a sketch, and after approval, I'll then finish the art. Payment is due at the start of the process. I will do refunds before the sketch has been sent. After the sketch is sent, refunds will not be available. Remeber that art is a luxury, and only make purchases you can afford.
  • Payment will be through ko-fi (uses PayPal).
  • Designs made by me MUST have a toyhouse. This is so I can keep track of them.
  • Designs made by me for this Art Game have limited commercial use. This means you can use them for your fursona, your mascot, and for small-scale commercial ventures. YouMAY NOT use my art for these ventures though. IT must be your own art.
  • Do not use my designs, especially not Starfield characters, for hateful or discriminatory actions.
