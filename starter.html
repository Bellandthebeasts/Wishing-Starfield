Here you'll enter into the world of Starfield!

First, choose one of your ocs that you think would enjoy a virtual pet like a webkinz, etc. Add that oc to this world as a Site User, with the Username you think they'd choose. We recommend making a tab on this character for starfield where you can record their escapades and user info for this ARPG.
Then, you will go here and get to know the castle. Don't forget to make a wish at the well! Then return here and go to #starter-prompts-submission in our discord, and answer a question about the wish and give us the username you want on our masterlist, we recommend your Toyhouse user, but please tell us where the username you provide is from.
After you answer those questions, you will recieve 500 Starbits, which is enough for a Downisaur MYO.

Got your Downisaur? Come finish part 2!
