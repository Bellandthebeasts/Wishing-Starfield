Currency Breakdown

We have three currencies in an effort to reward people
who draw many small pieces and those who draw fewer, more intense pieces.
Starbits: Starbits are earned via art. The content of the art determines how much you get!
Cookies: Each canvas you submit for rewards gives you one cookie!
Bells: Bells are earned by giving back to community or if you say something i find funny! These are used to buy art from me!

Art Scoring

The minimum requirement for art is a clean sketch of a headshot with color! Still fudging with numbers, but prizes are in Starbits!
Headshot: 50
Halfbody: 100
Fullbody: 150
Scene: 200
Gift Art: 50
Shading: 1.5x
Goofs, gags, and memes: 25 flat
NOTE ON GIFT ART: Gift art can be done of NON-STARFIELD characters as well, but this will get 30 starbits instead of 50!

Writing Scoring

Grading: Writing is graded on content, not skill or time spent, All Writing must consist of at least 250 words.
250 words: 50
+ 100 words: 25
At every 500 words starting at 750: 100
Poems will earn you 75

Additionally, each literature piece (one posting) you do will gain you one Cookie.
Multiple pieces in an anthology will each gain one Cookie.
